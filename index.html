Hi, I'm Viktor Stenby Johansson!

Young Academy Panel at the Danish Data Science Academy, currently studying MSc. Mathematical Modelling and Computation.

I'm an aspiring data scientist, who loves to learn about the world through data and mathematics. I have a bachelors degree from the Technical University of Denmark in Applied Mathematics and currently on my masters in Mathematical Modelling and Computation.

My area of interests are machine learning, data science and deep learning. In particular, I enjoy the fact that machine learning and data science can be found pretty much in every research area, and that this field is very often cross-disciplinary. I mean - large language models used for enzyme classification? Who would've thought?

On this website, I have gathered some of the data science tips and tricks that I have collected thus far! I hope to add more along the way, and please feel free to reach out if you find any errors or have any questions - I'd be happy to engage!

Happy reading!
